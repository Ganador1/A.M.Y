# 🔄 Autonomous Pipelines

Pipelines in the Autonomous System define the sequence of steps required to execute a complex scientific task. They ensure reproducibility and robustness in automated discovery.

## Standard Pipeline Structure

1.  **Initialization**: Setup of environment and resources.
2.  **Hypothesis Generation**: Creation of initial theories or targets.
3.  **Experiment Design**: Planning the validation strategy.
4.  **Execution**: Running simulations or interfacing with lab equipment (via Digital Twins).
5.  **Analysis**: Processing results.
6.  **Verification**: Cross-checking with established models.
