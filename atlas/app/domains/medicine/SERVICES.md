# Services in Medicine Domain

## AlphaFold3ProteinStructureService
- **Description**: Predicts protein structures using AlphaFold3.
- **Key Features**: Structure prediction, folding analysis.
- **Usage**: Via alphafold3 router.

## ClinicalBERTService
- **Description**: Processes clinical text with BERT.
- **Key Features**: Entity recognition, sentiment analysis in medical texts.
- **Usage**: Clinicalbert router.

## PersonalizedMedicineService
- **Description**: Provides personalized treatment recommendations.
- **Key Features**: Pharmacogenomics, risk assessment.
- **Usage**: Personalized medicine router.

## BiomechanicsService
- **Description**: Models biomechanical systems.
- **Key Features**: Movement analysis, musculoskeletal modeling.
- **Usage**: Integrated in unified router.

## MedicalImagingService
- **Description**: Processes medical images.
- **Key Features**: Segmentation, analysis of DICOM/NIfTI.
- **Usage**: Unified router.

## GenomicsService
- **Description**: Analyzes genomic data.
- **Key Features**: Variant calling, sequencing analysis.
- **Usage**: Unified router.
