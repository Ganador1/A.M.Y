# Services in Engineering Domain

## AdvancedLabAutomationService
- **Description**: Automates complex lab protocols.
- **Key Features**: PCR, ELISA, DNA extraction.
- **Usage**: Biomedical engineering.

## LabAutomationService
- **Description**: Basic automation for lab tasks.
- **Key Features**: Sample handling, data collection.
- **Usage**: General lab automation.

## SynthesisEquipmentService
- **Description**: Controls synthesis equipment.
- **Key Features**: Reaction monitoring, scheduling.
- **Usage**: Chemical engineering.

## ExperimentalToolkitHub
- **Description**: Central hub for experimental tools.
- **Key Features**: Tool listing, execution.
- **Usage**: Multi-domain experiments.

## ExperimentalValidator
- **Description**: Validates experimental data.
- **Key Features**: Statistical tests, outlier detection.
- **Usage**: Data validation.

## LabEquipmentBridge
- **Description**: Interfaces with lab hardware.
- **Key Features**: Equipment status, task queuing.
- **Usage**: Hardware integration.
