# Services in Chemistry Domain

## ComputationalChemistryService
- **Description**: Molecular and quantum analysis.
- **Key Features**: Structure manipulation, DFT calculations, sequence analysis.
- **Usage**: Via computational_chemistry.py router.

## ChemMLService
- **Description**: ML for chemistry.
- **Key Features**: Property prediction, drug-likeness scoring.
- **Usage**: Integrated in discovery pipelines.

## AdvancedNMRService
- **Description**: NMR analysis.
- **Key Features**: Spectrum processing, peak assignment.
- **Usage**: Endpoints in relevant routers.

## MaterialsDiscoveryService
- **Description**: Materials screening.
- **Key Features**: Property prediction, high-throughput screening.
- **Usage**: Routers in materials.py.
